Partial transcript of remarks by Sen. Rand Paul (R-Kentucky) on same-sex marriage at the Fox News Republican presidential debate on Aug. 6, 2015: 
Question:
Rand Paul:
And if people have an opinion, it’s a religious opinion that is heartedly felt, obviously they should be allowed to practice that and no government should interfere with them. 
One of the things that really got to me was the thing in Houston where you had the government, the mayor actually trying to give the sermons of ministers. When the government tries to invade the church to enforce its opinion on marriage, that’s when it’s time to resist.
